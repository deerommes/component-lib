About Soulful Spirit Bar & Grill

Soulful Spirits stands as a cherished, family-run gem in the heart of our community. Established in 1995 by the dynamic duo, Lisa and Mark Thompson, the reins have since been passed to their dedicated offspring, Matt Thompson. Our mission is simple: to serve up delectable dishes and enticing drinks that leave our guests craving more.
Nestled in a cozy corner, Soulful Spirits Bar & Grill offers a welcoming haven where the weary find solace over a chilled pint and heartwarming meals. It's an ideal spot for both solo relaxation and family gatherings. We pride ourselves on maintaining a smoke-free environment, attracting a friendly and respectful crowd.
For those nights when you're more inclined to lounge in your loungewear, we've got you covered. With our delivery service extending up to a 10-mile radius, your favorite comfort meals are just a phone call away from arriving at your doorstep.
